# #english-note 
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}k=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}$$
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^{2}=\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6}$$
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}k^{3}=\frac{n^{2}(n+1)^{2}}{4} = \left( \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \right)^2=\left( \sum_{k=1}^{n} k \right)^2$$
$$\sum_{k=1}^{n}2^{k}=2(2^{n}-1)$$
